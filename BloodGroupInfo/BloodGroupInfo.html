Blood Donation Facts

  • One unit of blood (450ml) can save up to 3 lives
  • Healthy adults can donate every 3 months
  • Blood donation takes only 10-15 minutes
  • Your body replaces lost blood within 24-48 hours
  • O- is the universal donor, AB+ is universal receiver
  • Less than 5% of eligible donors donate annually
  • Blood cannot be manufactured, only donated
  • One accident victim can require up to 100 units

Who Can Donate?

  • Age: 18-65 years old
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Health: Good general health
  • Hemoglobin: Minimum 12.5 g/dL
  • Frequency: Every 3 months for whole blood
  • Avoid if: Recent illness, surgery, or tattoo
